Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Breezelle

How does the Breezelle compare to other premium hair dryers?

The Breezelle outperforms competitors with its 110,000 RPM motor (compared to typical 90,000 RPM), higher ion concentration, and patented SmartGuard™ scalp protection technology. It's also lighter at 425g and includes our unique magnetic attachment system.

What's included in the box?

Your Breezelle comes with the main unit, concentrator nozzle, diffuser attachment, styling comb attachment, heat-resistant travel case, user manual, and 2-year warranty card. During our launch period, you'll also receive the bonus $49 Magnetic Nozzle Kit with 3 additional styling attachments.

How does the SmartGuard™ technology protect my scalp?

Our SmartGuard™ system uses multiple NTC thermal sensors to monitor temperature 100 times per second. When the sensors detect temperatures approaching the threshold that could damage your scalp's moisture barrier (typically 60°C), the microprocessor automatically adjusts power to maintain the optimal 57°C "Golden Temperature" that dries quickly without damage.

Is the Breezelle suitable for all hair types?

Yes! The Breezelle was designed and tested across all hair types and textures. For curly, coily, or textured hair, we recommend using the diffuser attachment. For straight or wavy hair, the concentrator gives you precision control. The IonShield™ technology benefits all hair types by reducing frizz and enhancing shine.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 14-day risk-free trial. If you're not completely satisfied, simply return your Breezelle in its original packaging for a full refund. We'll even cover the return shipping costs. Additionally, all Breezelle hair dryers come with our 2-year hassle-free replacement warranty against manufacturing defects.
